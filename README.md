# Satyadev Chada Digital Business Card

A production-ready, mobile-first digital business card for Satyadev Chada, Founder & Director at Infinitum Network Solutions.

## Included

- Infinitum black/cyan brand design
- Tap to call, WhatsApp and email
- Downloadable vCard contact
- Native mobile sharing with clipboard fallback
- QR-code modal
- LinkedIn profile and office map
- SEO, Open Graph metadata, PWA manifest, robots and sitemap

## Edit profile information

All contact fields are maintained in `src/data/cards.ts`.

The profile image is stored as `public/images/satyadev.jpg.b64` and served through `/api/profile`.
